Play what?

The Play Dept. (The Play Department) dig through the bottom of the music world, find the gems that mostly unnoticed, hidden by the noise of industry. The Play Dept. is perfect for a non-genre-specific music lover, receptive listeners, open-minded people who thirst for new music and the one that thinks back-to-the-root movement is pointless bullshit.

We prefer to not give label and genre or give a rate to any music. Whatever kind of music it is, if it's good, it's here. Otherwise, it's not worth our and your attention.
