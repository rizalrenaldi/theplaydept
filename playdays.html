Play what?

The Play Dept. (The Play Department) dig through the bottom of music world, find the gems that mostly unnoticed, hidden by the noise of industry. The Play Dept. is perfect for non-genre-specific music lover, receptive listeners, open-minded people who thrists for new music and the one that think back-to-the-root movement is pointless bullshit.

We don't give rating to any music. If it's good, it's here. Otherwise it's not worth our and your attention.
